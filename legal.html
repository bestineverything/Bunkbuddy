Terms and Conditions

Welcome to Bunk Buddy. By accessing this web application, you agree to comply with and be bound by the following terms of use:

1. Acceptable Use

Bunk Buddy is an academic utility intended to help students organize their schedule and track attendance. It is not affiliated with the official university administration. You agree to use this tool responsibly.

2. Service Availability

We strive to keep the platform online, but we do not guarantee 100% uptime. Bunk Buddy may occasionally experience downtime due to changes in the upstream portal formatting.

3. Academic Integrity

This tool predicts "bunkable" classes. Do not use this tool to deliberately circumvent academic requirements. The platform developers are not responsible for any direct or indirect consequences resulting from missing classes based on the dashboard projections.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our utmost priority. Here is how we handle your data:

  • Local Storage: Your academic credentials (roll number, password) are strictly stored and encrypted locally on your personal device using localStorage.
  • No Centralized Password Database: We do not store, log, or transmit your credentials to our external databases. Authentication happens via an automated headless bridge entirely in-session.
  • Community Resources: When you choose to upload a document to the Community feed, your Roll Number may be recorded in our Supabase instance to track Bunk Points. No personally identifiable information (other than what you voluntarily upload) is exposed to other users.

Refund Policy

Bunk Buddy is primarily a free, community-driven tool.

  • Pro Subscriptions (If applicable): Since our core tool is completely free, there are currently no paid subscription tiers available.
  • Rewards & Points: Bunk Points (BP) are accrued by uploading community resources and have zero cash value. Claimable rewards (e.g., "Free Chai") are promotional and fulfilled at the sole discretion of the admins.

If you have further questions regarding our policies, please contact support@bunkbuddy.in.
